Pollen tree detector (from satellite images)

Building a tree map of an area is very intensive task, especially when the trees are covering large and inaccessible sites. Numerous tree maps are available, but they are incomplete. However, having a detailed tree map may help predicting seasonal pollen spread in cities. Why is this important? Airborne pollen causes seasonal allergies. The number of people having allergic reactions increases on yearly basis. Remote collection of such information can be made possible through the utilization of satellite imagery. This project presents an automated tree detecting and identification method based on image processing of satellite imagery.
